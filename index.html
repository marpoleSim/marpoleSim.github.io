SERVICES

Analysis

We provide engineering analysis to support product design and development. Our analysis includes

  • Mechanical structural analysis
  • Fluid dynamics analysis for pressure drop, heat transfer, multi-phase and reactive fluid flow
  • Large engineering data analysis
  • Chemical engineering analysis
  • System simulation analysis

Project

We carry out your project from beginning to end. You get a complete analysis report. The process is simple. There is no need to purchase and maintain expensive commercial software and high performance computers. And, no complex hiring and training is needed for retaining specialists and/or IT supports. We cover these.

Support

Analysis has several components: problem formulation, classic hand calculation, sophisticated computer modelling. We support you in one or more of them to free up your work force, add resources, balance peak time workload, and introduce foreign expertise. We also support you with programming using object oriented language such as c++ or python and provide web-based calculation solution using Django.

ABOUT

marpoleSim provides services of scientific and engineering calculations including mechanics, heat transfer, fluid mechanics and chemical reaction.

Charles Li is the principal engineering analyst with industry experience of over 18 years. His area of expertise is computer modelling for engineering problems such as heat transfer, fluid dynamics and mechanical structural integrity and failure.

From 1998 to 2017, he worked as a CFD scientist & engineering analyst at Westport Fuel System Inc. in Canada. From 1995 to 1998, he was research associate and research assistant in University of Wisconsin-Madison and Hong Kong Polytechnic University. And, from 1993 to 1996, he taught as associate professor in Xi'an Jiaotong University.

Charles has a PhD degrees in engineering and a B.S degree in mathematics from Xi'an Jiaotong University, China.
